Our Kaiserbräu Selection

Kaiser Weisse (available as bottle or can)

A lot abundant and creamy foam, optimal adhesion, thickness and duration. Yellow gold color, hazy and extremely bubbly. Fine and extended fizz, much lively one. Elegant and typically weiss aspect. Balanced and prickly scent; notes of lemon, fresh corn and malty. Extremely stinging and racy taste. Determined notes of lemon, lightly acidulous on the end. Harmonious and well balanced.

Kaiser Pils

Pours a clear yellow golden color, topped with a nice looking and fairly lasting white frothy head. Malty aroma with some pleasant fresh grassy hops. Taste is sweet at first, grassy with some lemony feel in the body. Moderate hoppy bitterness in the finish. A pils worthy of its name.

Our Veldensteiner Selection

Veldensteiner Weissbier

Tangy - mild Weissbier with fine yeast. The distinctive, fruity flavor, highlighted with a slight banana note, guarantees a particularly refreshing taste

Veldensteiner Weissbier Dunkel

The use of local ingredients, the devotion to the traditional art of brewing and maintaining high quality standards give this specialty `dark wheat beer` its characteristic taste. A delight for lovers of good wheat beer.

Veldensteiner Premium Pils

Pours aclear straw yellow body with a nice two finger fizzy white head that slowly gained density. The nose is grassy hops with crusty bread pils malt and just a slight tinge of sulfur. Crisp flavor with a good bit of grassy hops and light bready maltiness. A bit of a carbonic bite on the finish and some lingering light bitterness. Light bodied and crisp with sturdy biting carbonation. Overalla highly delightful German Pilsener.

Veldensteiner Landbier (available as bottle or can)

A beer of the highest quality, sophistication and versatility. An impeccable balance and great flavors. Highly enjoyable on its own or with food. Light toasted nose, rich amber color, low tight head. Roasted malt body with a very distinct metallic texture and crisp edges. Nearly a smoke beer. Lingering hop bitter.

Veldensteiner Orginal Lager

Nice slightly hazy golden coloured body with a frothy white head - tons of bubbles rise from the bottom as well, which makes for a nice look! Aroma of refreshing malt, citrus, yeast, wheat and some fresh cut grass. Medium-bodied; Very fruity citrus flavours roaming through this one with a bit of a floral essence and finishes with a clean malt - very nice. Aftertaste is crisp and refreshing, not much hops, but plenty of smooth malt. Overall, a nice and potent helles great for the summertime.
